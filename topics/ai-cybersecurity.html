Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming cybersecurity by enabling faster detection of threats, anomaly detection, and automated response. However, it also introduces risks such as AI-powered attacks.

Applications of AI

  • Threat detection using machine learning.
  • Behavioral analysis of users and devices.
  • Automated incident response with AI-driven SOAR tools.
  • Fraud detection in banking and finance.

Risks

  • Adversarial AI that manipulates detection models.
  • AI-powered malware and phishing campaigns.
  • Data poisoning attacks against ML models.

Best Practices

  • Combine AI with human oversight (Human-in-the-Loop).
  • Regularly retrain models with verified data.
  • Use explainable AI (XAI) for transparency in decision-making.
