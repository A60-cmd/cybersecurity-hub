Overview

Mobile security protects smartphones and tablets from threats like malware, data theft, and unauthorized access. As mobile devices handle sensitive personal and business data, they are prime targets for attackers.

Common Risks

  • Malicious mobile apps disguised as legitimate ones.
  • Unsecured Wi-Fi connections leaking data.
  • Phishing through SMS or messaging apps.
  • Lost or stolen devices exposing sensitive data.

Best Practices

  • Install apps only from official app stores.
  • Enable device encryption and biometric locks.
  • Regularly update OS and apps.
  • Use Mobile Device Management (MDM) in organizations.
