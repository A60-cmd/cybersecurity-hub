Overview

Cybersecurity offers diverse career paths across technical, analytical, and leadership roles. With the rise of cybercrime, professionals are in high demand globally.

Popular Roles

  • Penetration Tester: Simulates attacks to find weaknesses.
  • Security Analyst: Monitors systems for suspicious activity.
  • Incident Responder: Handles breaches and minimizes damage.
  • Forensic Expert: Investigates digital evidence of crimes.
  • CISO (Chief Information Security Officer): Leads organizational security strategy.

Certifications

  • CompTIA Security+
  • Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)
  • Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)
  • Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP)

Growth Trends

According to Cybersecurity Ventures, there will be 3.5 million unfilled cybersecurity jobs globally by 2025, making it a lucrative and secure career choice.
